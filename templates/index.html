Autism Spectrum Disorder Prediction

Fill in the form to get a prediction based on behavioral and demographic features

About the Tool

This application uses machine learning to predict the likelihood of Autism Spectrum Disorder based on behavioral traits and demographic information.

The model has been trained on the Autism Screening dataset, which contains information about individuals with and without ASD diagnosis.

Note: This tool is for educational purposes only and should not be used for medical diagnosis. Please consult with a healthcare professional for proper evaluation.

How to Use

1. Enter your behavioral scores and demographic information in the form.

2. Click the "Predict" button to see your results.

3. The model will calculate a probability score indicating the likelihood of ASD.

4. Results are displayed along with a visual representation of the probability.

5. You can also explore the "Data Insights" section to learn more about the dataset.
